Hi, welcome to FeelGood!

Here you can donate to your favourite charity by doing tasks that are needed by others.

Don't feel like joining a charity that takes up to much of your time? Do you want to make a positive charitable contribution but perhaps lack the time to commit long term to your charity?

Well, we at FeelGood© believe we have just the solution. Find jobs posted by other users who pledge to give an amount to a charity of your choice on job completion. Or post a job yourself that you need doing and set an amount to pledge. Simple, right?

Feel free to register and log in to explore our website! and all the wondrous possibilities to make a difference!
